Our Story

Discover the tradition and passion behind our biryani

Traditional Recipes, Modern Presentation

Founded in 2005, Royal Biryani House has been serving authentic, flavorful biryani that captures the essence of traditional cooking while embracing modern culinary techniques.

Our master chefs use only the finest ingredients, including premium basmati rice, tender meats, and a secret blend of 15 aromatic spices that have been perfected over decades.
